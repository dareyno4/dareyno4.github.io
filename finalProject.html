My Childhood

I was born and raised in Chicago, Illinois for about 9 years of my life. In 2013, my mother enlisted in the U.S. Military, and was then station in the town of Colorado Springs, Colorado. From there, I spent the rest of my adolescence in Colorado Springs. I am the eldest of 5 siblings, and in 2022, I graduated from Mesa Ridge High School. I then went on to enroll at Arizona State University where I am currently studying Computer Science.
